Arke Industries

We make strategy games people love. We're currently working on Starfall, our next game. It's going to be big!

Our work

Our work to date. Some are prototypes, others are complete games. They all run, but most only for historic purposes.

Starfall

Starfall is our next big game. It's going to be a series of three games all set in the same universe at different time periods targeted at a variety of play styles.

  • Starfall: Midnight Contingency (In progress, 2016)
  • Starfall: Solar Reach (In progress, 2016)
  • Starfall: Winter Wars (In progress, 2016)
  • Regnum Interrus (Prototype, 2013)
  • Map Prototype (Prototype, 2012)

Silicon Dawn

Silicon Dawn was our second game. It featured an expansive map of the galaxy that players, as AIs, built upon and conquered.

  • Silicon Dawn (Release, 2010)
  • Map Prototype (Prototype, 2010)
  • Silicon Dawn Earth 2 (Prototype, 2010)
  • Silicon Dawn Earth 1 (Prototype, 2010)
  • Project Aurora (Prototype, 2009)

The Fall

The Fall was our very first game. We taught ourselves a lot when making it. It was a simple text-based game that simulated an empire.

  • The Fall Reimagined (Release, 2015)
  • The Fall 2 (Prototype, 2009)
  • The Fall (Release, 2009)
  • The Fall Alpha (Prototype, 2008)
